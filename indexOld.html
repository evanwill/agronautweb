« The Vandal Voice since 1889! »

For more than one hundred years the Argonaut has been the independent student publication of the University of Idaho. Reporting on the news of campus and the world, its been the Vandal Voice since 1898!

This collection provides free access to nearly 6,000 issues digitized from archival copies held by Special Collections and Archives. Issues can be browsed by year or searched in our database.

The Arg is produced by UI students and the Department of Student Involvement Student Media.
